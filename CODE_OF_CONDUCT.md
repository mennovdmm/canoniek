# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge
We pledge to make participation in Canon a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of background,
identity, or level of experience.

## Our standards
Behavior that contributes to a positive environment includes being respectful, welcoming differing viewpoints,
accepting constructive feedback gracefully, and focusing on what is best for the community.

Unacceptable behavior includes harassment, insulting or derogatory comments, personal or political attacks, and
publishing others' private information without permission.

## Scope
This Code of Conduct applies within all project spaces and when an individual is representing the project in
public spaces.

## Enforcement
Instances of abusive or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be reported to the project maintainers by opening an
issue or contacting the maintainers through the repository. Maintainers will review and respond, and will
respect the privacy of the reporter.

This Code of Conduct is adapted from the [Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org).
